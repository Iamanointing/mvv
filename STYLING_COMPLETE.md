## Responsive Breakpoints

- **Mobile:** < 640px (sm)
- **Tablet:** 640px - 1024px (sm, md, lg)
- **Desktop:** > 1024px (lg, xl)

---

## Typography

- **Headings:** Primary color (#0B3C5D), bold, responsive sizes
- **Body text:** Secondary color (#6C757D)
- **Labels:** Primary color, semibold
- **Responsive:** Scales from mobile (text-sm) to desktop (text-base/lg)
